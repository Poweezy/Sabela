Why It Matters

Climate change is not a distant threat; it's a present reality in Eswatini. We are experiencing rising temperatures, erratic rainfall, and more frequent droughts and floods, which impact our agriculture, water security, and unique ecosystems.

The Challenge

Climate change disproportionately affects the most vulnerable among us, including women, rural communities, the poor, and the youth. We fight for climate justice to ensure that no one is left behind.

The Opportunity

By investing in a green economy, renewable energy, and sustainable agriculture, we can unlock new opportunities for growth and prosperity while protecting our environment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our work and how you can get involved in building a sustainable Eswatini.

What is The Climate Watch?

The Climate Watch is an Eswatini-based NGO dedicated to combating climate change through community-driven action, advocacy, education, and sustainable development initiatives. We focus on building resilience and promoting climate justice across all communities in Eswatini.

How can I get involved?

There are many ways to join our mission! You can volunteer for our programs, participate in awareness campaigns, donate to support our work, join our newsletter for updates, or partner with us through corporate social responsibility initiatives. Visit our Get Involved section for more details.

What are the main climate challenges in Eswatini?

Eswatini faces rising temperatures, erratic rainfall patterns, increased frequency of droughts and floods, and threats to agriculture and water security. These challenges disproportionately affect rural communities, women, and vulnerable populations, impacting food security and livelihoods.

How is the organization funded?

We rely on donations from individuals, grants from international organizations, and partnerships with corporations committed to climate action. All contributions go directly toward our programs and initiatives. We are committed to transparency and regularly report on our impact.

How can I donate?

You can make a donation through our website by clicking the "Donate Now" button. We accept various payment methods and offer options for one-time or recurring donations. Every contribution, no matter the size, helps us continue our vital work in Eswatini.

Do you offer volunteer opportunities?

Yes! We welcome volunteers with skills in agriculture, education, advocacy, technology, community organizing, and more. Fill out our volunteer signup form in the Get Involved section, and we'll match you with opportunities that align with your interests and availability.
