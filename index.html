Who We Are

We are an Eswatini-based climate change NGO, grounded in local realities and communities. Our mission is to drive climate action through advocacy, education, and policy change, focusing on adaptation and mitigation strategies that align with local priorities. Our core values are climate justice, scientific integrity, equity, locally-led solutions, collaboration, and transparency.

What We Do

Adaptation & Resilience

We work with communities to build resilience to climate change through nature-based solutions and disaster risk reduction.

Sustainable Livelihoods

We promote sustainable livelihoods and community resilience by supporting climate-smart agriculture and other green initiatives.

Clean Energy

We advocate for a transition to clean and renewable energy sources to reduce greenhouse gas emissions.

GHG Emissions Reduction

We work to reduce greenhouse gas emissions from local sources like agriculture, land use, and energy.

Education & Awareness

We empower communities with knowledge through education, awareness campaigns, and capacity building.

Advocacy

We influence policy at national and regional levels to ensure that Eswatini's climate goals are ambitious and aligned with global commitments.

Why It Matters

Climate change is not a distant threat; it's a present reality in Eswatini. We are experiencing rising temperatures, erratic rainfall, and more frequent droughts and floods, which impact our agriculture, water security, and unique ecosystems. Climate change disproportionately affects the most vulnerable among us, including women, rural communities, the poor, and the youth. We fight for climate justice to ensure that no one is left behind. Inaction on climate change comes at a high cost. By investing in a green economy, renewable energy, and sustainable agriculture, we can unlock new opportunities for growth and prosperity. How We Work

Our approach is locally led and community-driven. We blend scientific knowledge with indigenous and local wisdom to create evidence-based solutions that work for our communities. We believe in the power of collaboration. We partner with government agencies, civil society organizations, research institutions, and international allies to maximize our impact. We are committed to transparency and accountability. We openly share our progress, challenges, and financial information to build trust with our partners and supporters.
